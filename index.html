FFXIV Class Types

DPS

DPS classes include the monk, samurai, ninja, black mage, bard, machinist, and dragoon. These classes focus on dealing as much damage as possible while avoiding damage.

TANK

Tank classes include gunbreaker, paladin, warrior, and dark knight. These classes focus on generating enmity and taking damage for the team.

HEALER

Healer classes include white mage, scholar, and astrologian. These classes focus on healing their teammates and protecting them from damage.
